    Description

    Steamed chicken with soya sauce

    Ingredients

    Sauce

    • Dark soy sauce 2/3 cup (140g)
    • Cooking wine 1/2 cup (90g)
    • Brown sugar 1/2 cup (80g)
    • Water 1 cup (180g)
    • Minced ginger 1/3 tbsp (5g)
    • Minced garlic 2 tbsp (30g)
    • Green onion 1 cup (80g)
    • Sesame oil 3 tbsp (20g)

    Jjimdak

    • 1 cut chicken (no. 12)
    • Water 1 cup (180g)
    • 1/2 potato (200g)
    • 1/2 carrot (100g)
    • Green onion 1/2 cup (40g)
    • 1 king oyster mushroom(60g)
    • 2 shiitake mushrooms (30g)
    • 3 Cheongyang pepper (20g)
    • 2 red pepper (15g)

    Directions

    1. Clean the chicken thoroughly and drain.
    2. Slice the green onion for garnish and cut diagonally. Finely chop the green onion for the sauce.
    3. Cut dried red pepper diagonally with scissors, and cut Cheongyang pepper into large pieces.
    4. Slice the red pepper diagonally, cut king oyster mushroom and shiitake mushroom in bite-size.
    5. Clean carrot, potato and sweet potato and cut them into bite-size.
    6. Mix dark soy sauce, brown sugar, water, minced ginger, minced garlic, green onion, and sesame oil to make the sauce.
    7. Put the chicken, sauce, and 1 cup of water in a pot and cook. When the chicken is somewhat cooked, add dried red pepper and continue cooking over high heat.
    8. Once the sauce boils, add potato, carrot, and sweet potato. When it boils, reduce the medium heat.
    9. When carrot, potato and sweet potato are cooked, add shiitake mushroom, king oyster mushroom, rice cake, and caramel.
    10. Once the chicken is seasoned thoroughly, add red pepper, Cheongyang pepper, and green onion and mix well.
